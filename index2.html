ABOUT US:

We are a company based in Stupnik, Zagreb, Croatia. We produce and install custom made furniture in Croatia (including islands), Slovenia and wider.
We can make all types of furniture and woodwork. We excell in producing elements made out of natural materials. Our specialties are massive kitchen elements, wooden stairways, and door made out of wood of classic or modern design according to your wish.
